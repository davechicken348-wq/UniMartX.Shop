General

Everything you need to know about UniMartX.

UniMartX is a platform that helps student entrepreneurs build professional online businesses without building their own websites. We give every student seller their own permanent storefront at unimartx.com/shop/your-brand, complete with product catalogue, order management, and customer reviews — all powered by campus visibility.

Yes — creating an account and browsing student shops is completely free. Student sellers currently pay 0% commission during our early launch period. Keep 100% of your earnings with no monthly fees, no listing fees, and no hidden charges. A small platform fee will be introduced later with advance notice.

Currently, UniMartX is live at Sunyani Technical University (STU). We're starting with student entrepreneurs in Sunyani before expanding to universities across Ghana. If your campus isn't listed yet, you can still register and build your online business.

Not yet — but our website is fully optimised for mobile browsers, so you get a near-app experience right from your phone. A dedicated Android and iOS app is on our roadmap and will be announced soon.

UniMartX is different from other marketplaces because we're not a marketplace — we're a platform for student entrepreneurs. Instead of competing to be the biggest mall, we give every student seller their own branded shop, their own link, and their own business identity — without requiring them to build a website. The marketplace simply helps buyers discover these student businesses.

Buying

Everything about placing orders and getting your items.

Browse the shop, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll enter your delivery details and choose a payment method. Once payment is confirmed, your order is sent to the seller and you'll receive a confirmation email with tracking details.

Yes. All sellers go through a verification process before they can list products. Payments are held securely and only released to the seller after you confirm receipt of your order. If something goes wrong, our buyer protection policy has you covered.

If your item doesn't arrive within the stated delivery window, contact the seller first through your order page. If the issue isn't resolved within 48 hours, open a dispute with our support team. We'll investigate and issue a full refund if the seller cannot confirm delivery.

Returns are handled on a per-seller basis. Each store has its own return policy displayed on their store page. Generally, items can be returned within 7 days if they arrive damaged, defective, or significantly different from the listing. Check the seller's policy before purchasing.

Once your order is confirmed, you'll receive a tracking link via email. You can also view your order status anytime from your account dashboard under "My Orders". The seller updates the status as your item is packed and dispatched.

Running Your Shop

Build your online business, manage orders, and get paid.

Click "Create My Shop" from the homepage and complete seller registration in under a minute. Choose your shop name, set up your storefront, add a banner and logo, then start listing products. You'll get your own unique link — unimartx.com/shop/your-brand — that you can share anywhere.

You can sell physical products and services — from fashion and electronics to baked goods, tutoring, design services, and more. The best part? You get your own branded shop instead of being buried in a crowded catalogue.

Payments are processed through your linked Mobile Money or bank account. Once a buyer confirms receipt of their order, funds are released to your seller wallet within 24 hours. You can withdraw to your registered account at any time from your seller dashboard.

UnimartX currently charges 0% commission during our early launch period — sellers keep 100% of their earnings. There are no listing fees, no monthly subscriptions, and no setup costs. A small platform fee will be introduced later with 30 days' advance notice.

Absolutely. UniMartX is built for student entrepreneurs everywhere — on campus and off. Whether you're a student with a side hustle or a solo founder, you get the same tools: your own storefront, your own link, order management, and full platform access.

Payments

Payment methods, security, and refund processes.

UnimartX accepts Mobile Money (MTN MoMo, Vodafone Cash, AirtelTigo Money), debit and credit cards (Visa, Mastercard), and direct bank transfers. We're continuously adding more payment options to make checkout as convenient as possible.

Yes. All payment data is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS protocols. We do not store your card or Mobile Money details on our servers. Payments are processed through certified payment gateways that comply with PCI-DSS security standards.

If your order qualifies for a refund (item not received, item significantly different from listing, or item arrived damaged), submit a refund request from your order page within 7 days of the expected delivery date. Approved refunds are processed within 3–5 business days back to your original payment method.

Cash on delivery is available for select sellers and delivery zones. If a seller supports it, you'll see "Pay on Delivery" as an option at checkout. Note that buyer protection only applies to orders paid through the platform — cash transactions are at your own risk.

Payment declines can happen for several reasons — insufficient funds, incorrect card details, network issues, or your bank blocking the transaction. Try a different payment method or contact your bank. If the issue persists after multiple attempts, reach out to our support team and we'll help resolve it.

Account

Managing your profile, security, and settings.

Click "Forgot password?" on the login page and enter your registered email address. You'll receive a reset link within a few minutes. The link expires after 15 minutes for security. If you don't see the email, check your spam folder or request a new link.

Not yet — currently, buyer and seller accounts are separate and require different email addresses to register. We're exploring dual-role support so one account can do both, but for now you'll need to choose the role that fits your needs at signup.

After registering, a verification email is sent to your inbox automatically. Click the link in the email to verify your account. If you didn't receive it, go to your account settings and click "Resend verification email". Unverified accounts have limited access to platform features.

You can request account deletion from your account settings under "Privacy & Security". Note that deleting your account is permanent — all your data, order history, and store information will be removed. If you have an active seller store, all pending payouts must be settled before deletion is processed.

Log in and go to your account dashboard, then click "Profile Settings". You can update your name, phone number, profile photo, and delivery address from there. Email address changes require re-verification for security purposes.
