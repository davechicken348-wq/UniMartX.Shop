Our Mission

Every student deserves a permanent place to sell.

Across campuses in Ghana, thousands of student entrepreneurs run real businesses — selling fashion, electronics, baked goods, tech accessories, and services. Yet most rely on WhatsApp Status updates that vanish in 24 hours, Instagram posts buried by algorithms, or chaotic Telegram groups.

UniMartX was built to fix this. Every student seller gets a permanent, professional online storefront — complete with a branded catalogue, order management, customer reviews, and a shareable link. No coding. No monthly fees. Just one link that turns your hustle into a real brand.

Our Story

From a dorm room to a live platform.

A developer and a digital marketer, one laptop, and a lot of late nights — turning a campus problem into a platform that's live today.

The Problem — 2024

A frustrating reality

Students at Sunyani Technical University were running real businesses — but only through disappearing WhatsApp Status posts and chaotic Instagram DMs. There was no permanent shop, no easy way to discover sellers, and no professional way to manage orders. We decided to do something about it.

Building — 2025

From idea to code

We built the first version of UniMartX together — Node.js, Express, and PostgreSQL. Fast, reliable, and ready to scale. Every feature was designed around one thing: giving student sellers a shop they could be proud of.

Launch — 2026

Going live at STU

UniMartX officially launched at Sunyani Technical University. Student sellers who had been posting on WhatsApp Status now had a permanent, professional shop with their own unique link. The platform is live, real, and growing.

Growing — Now

Expanding across campuses

New sellers join every week, the product catalog keeps expanding, and the platform is evolving fast. A mobile app is on the way — because student businesses shouldn't be limited to desktop screens.

Our Values

What drives everything we build.

These aren't just words on a wall — they're the decisions that shaped every feature on UniMartX.

Student-first, always

Every feature is designed with student sellers and buyers in mind. Campus life, limited budgets, the hustle — we live it too.

Real brand identity

No generic catalogue. Every student gets their own branded shop, their own link, and their own space to build something lasting.

Zero friction to start

0% commission. No setup fees. No monthly subscriptions. No hidden charges. Open a shop, list products, and start selling — all for free.

Trust by design

Verified sellers, secure payments, buyer protection, and real customer reviews. Safeguards built in so both sides can transact with confidence.

Community over competition

UniMartX isn't about being the biggest mall — it's about lifting every student entrepreneur. Every shop, every sale, every new seller counts.

Accessible everywhere

The website works beautifully on any phone. A dedicated mobile app is on the way — because student businesses shouldn't be stuck on desktop.
