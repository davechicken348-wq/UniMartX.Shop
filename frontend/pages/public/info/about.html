Our Story

It started with a frustrating problem.

This isn't a corporate origin story. It's the beginning of a movement — told the way it actually happened.

  1. The Problem

    Talented, but invisible.

    Students were running real businesses — fashion, tech, food, crafts — but only through WhatsApp Status that vanishes in 24 hours and DMs nobody scrolls back to find.

  3. The Idea

    One link. One store.

    What if every student seller had a permanent, professional shop — their own branded space, one shareable link, and zero code required to set it up?

  5. The Solution

    UniMartX was built.

    We built the platform we wished we had — storefronts, catalogs, orders, and reviews, designed around campus life and made free for every student.

  7. The Future

    And it's just beginning.

    New sellers join every week. Campuses are connecting. The dream is simple: a thriving marketplace owned by students, for students.

Why We Exist

Student entrepreneurs face real walls.

Before UniMartX, selling as a student meant fighting the same four problems — every single day.

Hard to reach buyers

Your products only reach whoever happens to see a fleeting story. Real reach shouldn't depend on luck.

Stuck on WhatsApp

Orders live in chat threads. There's no catalog, no tracking, no way to look professional or stay organized.

Trust is difficult

Buyers worry about scams. Without reviews or verified shops, every transaction feels risky.

Limited discoverability

Great stores stay hidden. There was no central place where students could browse what their peers sell.

So we built the solution.

UniMartX tears down those walls — giving every student seller a real storefront, verified trust, and a community of campus buyers ready to discover them.
