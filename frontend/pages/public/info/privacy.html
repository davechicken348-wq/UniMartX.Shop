This Privacy Policy explains how UniMartX collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our platform to build your student business, shop from student entrepreneurs, or manage your online storefront. By using UniMartX, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

01

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us and information generated through your use of the platform.

Information you provide:

  • Account details — name, email address, phone number, and password.
  • Profile information — profile photo and delivery address.
  • Seller information — store name, category, description, and location.
  • Payment information — processed securely through our payment partners. We do not store card or Mobile Money details.
  • Communications — messages sent through our support system or between sellers and buyers.

Information collected automatically:

  • Device information — browser type, operating system, and device identifiers.
  • Usage data — pages visited, search queries, stores and products viewed, and time spent on the platform.
  • Transaction data — order history, payment amounts, and delivery details.
  • IP address and approximate location data.
02

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Facilitate transactions and process payments between buyers and sellers through our platform.
  • Send order confirmations, shipping updates, and account notifications.
  • Provide customer support and resolve disputes.
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents.
  • Improve and personalise your experience on the platform.
  • Send promotional emails and platform updates — you can opt out at any time.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

We will never sell your personal data to third parties for their own marketing purposes.

03

Information Sharing

We share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With student shops: When a buyer places an order, we share their name, delivery address, and contact details with the seller to fulfil the order.
  • With buyers: When a buyer places an order with a student shop, we share the seller's store details and the buyer's delivery information with both parties to complete the transaction.
  • Payment processors: When automated payment features are live, we share necessary transaction data with certified payment partners to process payments securely. These providers are contractually bound to protect your data.
  • Service providers: We work with trusted third-party providers for hosting, analytics, and email delivery. These providers are contractually bound to protect your data.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of UnimartX and its users.

We do not share your personal information with advertisers or data brokers. UniMartX is a platform for student entrepreneurs — we do not sell your data to third parties for marketing purposes.

04

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure servers protected by industry-standard security measures including:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data in transit.
  • Encryption at rest for sensitive data.
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments.
  • Strict access controls — only authorised personnel can access user data.

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymise your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we take every reasonable precaution, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

05

Cookies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on UnimartX. Cookies are small text files stored on your device.

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the platform to function — login sessions, cart contents, and security tokens.
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how users interact with the platform so we can improve it.
  • Preference cookies: Remember your settings and preferences for a better experience.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may affect platform functionality. We do not use cookies for third-party advertising.

06

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information through your account settings.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and personal data.
  • Portability: Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time using the link in any email we send.
  • Restriction: Request that we limit how we process your data in certain circumstances.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at dexecommerce2@gmail.com or through our support form. We will respond within 30 days.

07

Children's Privacy

UnimartX is not intended for children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 16. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 16 without parental consent, we will delete that information immediately.

If you believe a child under 16 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at dexecommerce2@gmail.com.

08

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we make significant changes, we will:

  • Notify you by email to your registered address.
  • Display a notice on the platform.
  • Update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Your continued use of UnimartX after changes take effect constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
