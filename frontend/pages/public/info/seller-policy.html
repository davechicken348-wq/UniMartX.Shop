This Seller Policy governs all student entrepreneurs and sellers on the UniMartX platform. By registering as a seller or listing products, you agree to comply with this policy in addition to our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. We're committed to helping you build your business — these rules keep the platform fair and trustworthy for every student entrepreneur.

01

Seller Eligibility

To sell on UnimartX, you must:

  • Be at least 16 years of age.
  • Have a valid, verified UnimartX account.
  • Provide accurate personal or business information during registration.
  • Have a valid Mobile Money account or bank account for receiving payouts.
  • Not be currently suspended or banned from the platform.

UniMartX supports two seller types:

  • Campus Sellers — students or staff selling within a university or institution. A valid student or staff ID may be required for verification.
  • Independent Sellers — individuals or small businesses selling outside campus. Business registration documents may be requested for verification.

All sellers — regardless of type — get their own branded storefront at unimartx.com/shop/your-brand, their own shop link, and access to the same platform tools.

02

Store Registration

To open a store on UnimartX, you must complete the seller registration process which includes:

  • Providing your full name, email address, and phone number.
  • Selecting your seller type (campus or independent).
  • Choosing a unique store name and category.
  • Writing an accurate store description.
  • Providing your store location (country and city).
  • Agreeing to the Terms of Service, Privacy Policy, and this Seller Policy.

UniMartX reserves the right to reject any store registration that does not meet our standards or contains false information. Approved stores are typically live within 24 hours of submission. Remember: your store is your brand. Make it count.

Your store name must not infringe on any trademarks or impersonate another business or individual. UnimartX may require you to change a store name that violates this rule.

03

Product Listings

All product listings on UnimartX must meet the following standards:

  • Accuracy: Titles, descriptions, and images must accurately represent the item being sold. Misleading listings are strictly prohibited.
  • Images: At least one clear, original photo of the actual item is required. Stock images may be used only if the item is brand new and sealed.
  • Pricing: Prices must be clearly stated in Ghanaian Cedis (GH₵). Hidden fees or charges not disclosed in the listing are not permitted.
  • Condition: The condition of the item (new, used — good, used — fair) must be accurately stated.
  • Availability: Only list items you currently have in stock and can fulfil. Remove or update listings when items are no longer available.
  • Category: Items must be listed in the correct category. Miscategorised listings may be removed without notice.

UnimartX reserves the right to remove any listing that does not comply with these standards without prior notice.

04

Prohibited Items

The following items are strictly prohibited on UnimartX and may not be listed under any circumstances:

  • Counterfeit, replica, or unauthorised copies of branded goods.
  • Weapons, firearms, ammunition, explosives, or dangerous materials.
  • Illegal drugs, controlled substances, or drug paraphernalia.
  • Stolen goods or items obtained through illegal means.
  • Adult content, sexually explicit material, or pornography.
  • Live animals or products derived from endangered species.
  • Prescription medications without proper authorisation.
  • Alcohol or tobacco products.
  • Hazardous chemicals or materials.
  • Items that infringe on intellectual property rights.
  • Digital goods, software licences, or gift cards.
  • Services of any kind — UniMartX is a platform for physical goods and student businesses. Service listings may be considered on a case-by-case basis for student entrepreneurs.

Listing prohibited items will result in immediate store suspension and may be reported to the relevant authorities. If you are unsure whether an item is permitted, contact us before listing.

05

Order Fulfilment

When a buyer places an order with your store, you are obligated to:

  • Confirm the order within 24 hours of receiving it.
  • Pack and dispatch the item within your stated processing time (maximum 3 business days unless otherwise agreed).
  • Update the order status on your seller dashboard when the item is dispatched.
  • Provide accurate tracking information where available.
  • Communicate proactively with the buyer if there are any delays.

Repeated failure to fulfil orders on time will result in a warning, followed by store suspension if the behaviour continues. Orders that are not confirmed within 48 hours may be automatically cancelled and refunded to the buyer.

You are responsible for the safe packaging and delivery of your items. Damage caused by poor packaging is the seller's responsibility and may result in a refund being issued to the buyer.

06

Pricing & Fees

UnimartX charges 0% commission during our early launch period. This means sellers keep 100% of their earnings. There are no listing fees, monthly subscriptions, or setup costs. A small platform fee will be introduced later with advance notice.

The commission is automatically deducted from the sale amount before funds are released to your seller wallet. For example, if you sell an item for GH₵100, you receive GH₵95.

You are free to set your own prices. However, UnimartX reserves the right to remove listings with prices that are:

  • Artificially inflated to manipulate search rankings or promotions.
  • Significantly below market value in a way that suggests fraudulent activity.
  • Inconsistent with the item's described condition or value.

UnimartX may introduce a platform fee with 30 days' notice to all active sellers.

07

Payouts

Current state (pre-escrow): During our early launch period, payments are arranged directly between buyers and sellers. UnimartX does not hold, process, or mediate payments on your behalf. You are responsible for agreeing on payment methods, timing, and terms directly with each buyer.

Post-launch (escrow): An automated escrow and payout system will be introduced after launch. All sellers will receive at least 30 days' advance notice before any automated payment or escrow system goes live, including details of any applicable platform fees.

Until the escrow system is live, you retain full control over payment arrangements with your buyers. Ensure you clearly communicate accepted payment methods, timing, and any delivery or service fees before fulfilling orders.

Once the escrow system is introduced, funds from completed sales will be held securely and released to sellers according to the terms communicated at launch. UnimartX will notify you of all changes well in advance.

08

Returns & Refunds

Refunds are mandatory. All sellers are required to process valid refund requests in a timely manner. Refusing, ignoring, or abandoning a legitimate refund obligation is a serious violation of this policy and will result in penalties including account suspension or permanent termination.

You must have a clearly stated return policy on your store page. At minimum, you must accept returns in the following situations:

  • The item was not delivered.
  • The item received is significantly different from the listing description or images.
  • The item arrived damaged or defective.

When a buyer opens a refund dispute, you are required to:

  • Respond within 48 hours of receiving the dispute notification.
  • Provide clear evidence to support your position (photos, tracking information, delivery confirmation, etc.).
  • Accept the refund if the buyer's claim is valid and supported by evidence.

Failure to respond within 48 hours will result in UnimartX issuing a full refund to the buyer automatically, and your account will receive a strike. Accumulating three strikes within a 90-day period will result in a 30-day suspension. A fourth strike will result in permanent termination.

Fraudulent or unjustified refund requests may be appealed by contacting UnimartX support with supporting evidence. UnimartX's decision in all disputes and appeals is final.

09

Seller Conduct

As a seller on UnimartX, you agree to maintain professional and ethical conduct at all times. The following behaviours are strictly prohibited:

  • Harassing, threatening, or abusing buyers or other sellers.
  • Attempting to conduct transactions outside the platform to avoid fees.
  • Sharing personal contact details (phone numbers, social media) in listings or messages to redirect buyers off-platform.
  • Creating multiple seller accounts to circumvent restrictions or bans.
  • Manipulating search rankings through keyword stuffing or false categorisation.
  • Offering incentives to buyers in exchange for positive reviews.
  • Posting false, misleading, or fraudulent listings.
  • Impersonating another seller, brand, or individual.

Attempting to redirect buyers off-platform is one of the most serious violations of this policy and will result in immediate permanent account termination.

10

Ratings & Reviews

After each completed transaction, buyers may leave a rating and review for your store. Ratings are calculated as an average of all reviews received.

You may not:

  • Offer discounts, refunds, or gifts in exchange for positive reviews.
  • Ask buyers to remove or change negative reviews.
  • Use fake accounts to leave reviews on your own store.
  • Threaten or pressure buyers regarding their reviews.

You may respond publicly to reviews on your store page. Responses must be professional and respectful. UnimartX reserves the right to remove responses that are abusive or violate our conduct standards.

Reviews that are found to be fraudulent, abusive, or in violation of our policies may be removed by UnimartX at our discretion.

11

Suspension & Termination

UnimartX reserves the right to suspend or permanently terminate any seller account for violations of this policy, the Terms of Service, or any applicable law.

Grounds for immediate suspension include:

  • Listing prohibited items.
  • Fraudulent activity or misrepresentation.
  • Attempting to redirect buyers off-platform.
  • Repeated failure to fulfil orders.
  • Abusive behaviour toward buyers or UnimartX staff.
  • Creating multiple accounts after a ban.

For less severe violations, we will issue a warning before taking action. Three warnings within a 90-day period will result in a 30-day suspension. A fourth violation will result in permanent termination.

Upon termination, any pending payouts will be processed after a 30-day review period to allow for dispute resolution. UnimartX is not obligated to pay out funds associated with fraudulent transactions.

You may appeal a suspension by contacting dexecommerce2@gmail.com within 14 days of the suspension notice.
